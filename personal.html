Swimming

Swimming has been a part of life since 6 years old. I joined the Fremont Flying Fish Swim Team with Coach Mark Carter when I was 7 years old and made my way from there to who I am today. Coach Mark was there the whole time helping me through the hard times and guiding me through each and every step. Not only does swimming help me with mind focus, but it also helps me to relieve stress. High School swimming has also been fabulous. Recently High School Swimming has also been a blast not only with the coaches, but with the teammates. I really love the team bondings and all the gatherings we do together. I look foward to another great season. Swimming will always be a part of my life.
